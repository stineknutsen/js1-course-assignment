This Privacy Policy explains how Square Eyes collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our website.

Opt-Out: You can opt-out of receiving marketing communications from us at any time.

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@squareeyes.com.
